Welcome, neighbors. You are welcome here in the home of Wat Tyler - man of Kent, friend of John Ball, rebel; and, in modern days, socialist, rebel, engineer. It is my firm belief that technology is a tool, not a savior, and that the capitalist class sells us the lie of technological salvation only as a means to control us; and that the only way forward is class warfare, which the wealthy have been waging against us for centuries. Whether technology is a tool we can put to use, or a tool we must put down - that remains to be seen.
